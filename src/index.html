About Us

Seed (noun - /siːd/) - a small, [round or oval] object produced by a plant and from which, when it is planted, a new plant can grow.

We are fruit of the Seed Challenge promoted by Tomorrow University on the first week of the Professional Master (PM) program on Sustainability, Entrepreneurship and Technology (SET). Tomorrow's Wave has been cultivated by "Team 5".

What's the purpose of a seed? Logically and simply, it is to birth a tree. But if you think about the value of a tree, you may find different meanings. It provides shadow so one can rest on a warm day. It provides shelter for birds on a stormy day. It may produce fruits. It protects rivers and lakes for natural development

Tomorrow's Wave is a taskforce that aims to maximize the power of community, by connecting like-minded change makers with environmentally friendly NGOs and use the communities' resources whether that be time, donations or wisdom to contribute to the development of the NGO’s cause.

How have we invested the seed money?

For our seed challenge we decided to look at how we could generate value from a small symbolic contribution and maximize the value of our collective actions. Therefore, we decided to invest the seed money offered to purchase a domain and establish Tomorrow's Wave.

Short-, and long-term goal

We will collect initial donations, and forward these to the NGO in one batch, trying to catch their attention, and initiate a partnership between the NGOs and our Cohort.

We aim to develop a platform where individuals can self-organize and partnership with multiple NGOs, investors, innovators and founders, where their causes are supported, and in return they offer their knowledge and expertise back to the community.

How does Tomorrow's Wave create value?

The NGOs benefits from the contributions of the community of change makers from our Cohort, Tomorrow University benefits, if and when, partnerships are made with even more NGOs. Our Cohort I8 benefits when we connect with NGOs and investors who can lecture and mentor on our program.

Community

Tomorrow’s Wave aims to maximize the power of the community, by bringing together like-minded people to make a sustainable impact.

Connect

Tomorrow’s Wave connects change makers with NGOs, investors and innovators through a programme of live webinars and challenges.

Contribute

Tomorrow’s Wave is also a portal to contribute resources to NGOs whether that is through donating time, money or wisdom.
